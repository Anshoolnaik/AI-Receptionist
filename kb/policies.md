# Hotel Policies

## Check-in / Check-out Times
- **Check-in time**: 2:00 PM (14:00)
- **Check-out time**: 11:00 AM (11:00)
- Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.

## Cancellation Policy
- Cancellations made 48+ hours before check-in: full refund
- Cancellations within 48 hours: first night charged as cancellation fee
- No-shows: full booking amount charged

## Booking Sources
Bookings can come from direct (walk-in/phone), OTA (Booking.com, MakeMyTrip, Agoda), or WhatsApp.

## Room Types
Standard, Deluxe, and Suite rooms are available depending on the property.

## Payment
Payments accepted in INR. Amount is stored as `amount_inr` in the system.
